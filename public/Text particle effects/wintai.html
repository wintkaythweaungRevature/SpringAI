Q.01
Does W!ntAi really publish directly — or just send me reminders?
Directly. We hold official API partnerships with all 8 platforms, so your video, caption, thumbnail and schedule ship the moment you hit Publish. No phone notifications, no "open the app to post" tricks.
+
Q.02
Will the AI sound like my brand or like ChatGPT?
Your brand. Upload a style guide, your last 100 posts, or a voice memo describing your tone. Our RAG layer grounds every generation in that corpus — and flags drift if the output starts sounding generic.
+
Q.03
I'm an agency with 22 clients. Can I keep brand voices separate?
Yes. Every workspace is sandboxed. Each client gets their own brand-voice vault, approval chain, and white-label reports. Seats and accounts scale with the Agency plan.
+
Q.04
What if a platform changes its algorithm or aspect ratio?
We re-tune the reframer + caption rules within 24 hours of any announced change, and we silently A/B test new rules against the old ones on our fleet before rolling them to you.

Can I cancel whenever?
Yes. Two clicks, no retention call. Your data exports as CSV + MP4 originals.
