About

What is WintAi?

WintAi is an AI-powered social media management platform that lets creators and agencies publish videos, schedule posts, and grow across eight platforms — YouTube, Instagram, TikTok, Facebook, LinkedIn, X (Twitter), Threads, and Pinterest — from a single dashboard. Upload one video, and WintAi adapts the format, writes captions in your brand voice, and publishes to every channel you've connected.

The product started from a simple frustration: running a personal brand across four or five social platforms means doing the same edit, caption, hashtag, and upload five times. Every platform has its own aspect ratio, caption length, hashtag convention, and posting API. Most existing tools solve a sliver of that problem — they schedule, but don't format; or they format, but don't write. WintAi is the full pipeline.

Who WintAi is for

Solo creators

If you're a YouTuber, TikTok creator, or Instagram-first brand and you want your content repurposed to every platform without paying an editor, WintAi is built for your workflow. Upload once, tick the platforms you want to post to, and WintAi handles the rest — including a per-platform caption rewrite tuned to your actual writing style (not a generic ChatGPT-sounding paraphrase).

Agencies and multi-brand managers

WintAi's Workspaces let one account run multiple clients with fully isolated brand voices, connected social accounts, calendars, analytics, and team permissions. Drop in three Facebook accounts, two YouTube channels, and any number of Instagrams per client — the publish flow lets you tick which specific account each post goes out from. Agencies on the Growth plan get unlimited connected accounts per platform.

Teams with non-technical members

The AI Workspace runs three AI agents every morning: a Strategist that picks the day's topic, a Writer that drafts captions in your brand voice, and an Analyst that reports on yesterday's performance. You approve with a click; the post goes live. Non-technical team members get a queue of approve / reject decisions — no blank page, no style drift.

How WintAi works

The flow has three steps end-to-end.

  1. Connect your accounts. Standard OAuth 2.0 flows for each platform. Free tier connects one account per platform; Pro supports three; Growth is unlimited. Accounts are a flat pool — any connected account can publish into any workspace.
  2. Create content. Upload a video, or describe an idea and let the Writer agent draft it. WintAi generates per-platform variants: vertical 9:16 for Reels, TikTok, and YouTube Shorts; horizontal for the main YouTube feed; square for Facebook and LinkedIn. Captions, hashtags, and hooks are generated per platform in your brand voice.
  3. Publish or schedule. Tick the accounts you want to post to (the "Post to" chip row shows all connected accounts grouped by platform). Hit Publish for immediate posting, or pick a time from the drag-and-drop calendar. WintAi tracks likes, comments, and views across every platform post-publish.

What makes WintAi different

  • Brand-voice AI, not generic AI. You upload a few sample posts; WintAi learns your sentence rhythm, emoji frequency, and slang — every generated caption matches it.
  • Multi-account-per-platform pool. Run three Facebook pages from one login. Buffer / Hootsuite charge per seat per account; WintAi charges per tier with a fixed account cap.
  • Self-Healing Content — the only tool on the market that detects underperforming posts, rewrites captions in your current voice, and auto-reschedules. Old posts become second-chance posts.
  • Live brand monitoring. The Brand Guardian scans Reddit and X in real time for mentions and sentiment-tags them — no database bloat, no 30-day history, just what's being said right now.
  • Deep analytics per workspace. Impressions, clicks, follows, engagement rate — all rolled up with AI commentary explaining why a post took off.

The tech

WintAi runs on a Spring Boot 3 backend hosted on AWS EC2 + RDS PostgreSQL, with AWS S3 for video storage and a React frontend on AWS Amplify. Every social platform is integrated via OAuth 2.0 + PKCE; access tokens refresh automatically, and tokens are scoped per connected account (not per workspace), so the same YouTube channel can publish into any number of workspaces. Platform integrations currently cover YouTube Data API v3, Meta Graph API (Facebook + Instagram), TikTok Login Kit + Content Posting API, LinkedIn Marketing API, X API v2, Threads API, and Pinterest v5.
