Quick Start

W!ntAi runs entirely in your browser — no installation required.

Step 1: Open the App

Visit wintaibot.com and click any tool tab at the top of the page.

Step 2: Create a Free Account

Click Sign Up in the header. Enter your email and password. Verify your email via the confirmation link.

Step 3: Start Using Free Tools

After verifying your email, you have immediate access to Ask AI and Recipe Generator — no payment required.

Step 4: Upgrade for Full Access

To use DocuWizard, EchoScribe, Image Generator, Reply Enchanter, and Resume Warlock, go to Account Settings → Upgrade (paid plans from $19/month via Stripe; annual options available).

Account & Billing

Managing Your Subscription

Go to Account tab (visible when logged in) to:

  • View your current plan (Free or Member)
  • Upgrade to a paid plan (Starter, Pro, or Growth)
  • Cancel your subscription (access continues until period end)
  • View invoices and update payment method via Stripe Billing Portal
  • Deactivate your account
Payments are processed by Stripe. W!ntAi does not store your bank account or full card details — only Stripe handles that payment information.

Ask AI Free

Ask AI is W!ntAi's general-purpose AI chatbot. Use it for questions, writing, coding, research, and more.

How to Use

1. Click the Ask AI tab → 2. Type your question or request → 3. Press Enter or click Send

Example Prompts

  • "Explain how React hooks work in simple terms"
  • "Write a professional bio for my LinkedIn profile"
  • "Debug this Python code: [paste code]"
  • "What are the main differences between REST and GraphQL?"
  • "Summarize the key points from this text: [paste text]"

Recipe Generator Free

Enter ingredients you have and get complete recipes with instructions.

How to Use

1. Click Recipe Generator → 2. List your ingredients (comma-separated) → 3. Optionally specify dietary preferences → 4. Click Generate

Tips

  • Include pantry staples (oil, salt, garlic) for more realistic recipes
  • Specify "vegetarian", "under 30 minutes", or "high protein" for tailored results

DocuWizard – PDF & Document AI Member

Upload PDF, Excel, or Word documents for AI-powered extraction and analysis.

Supported File Types

  • PDF (.pdf)
  • Excel (.xlsx, .xls)
  • Word (.docx)

How to Use

1. Click DocuWizard → 2. Upload your file (drag & drop or browse) → 3. Ask a question or request a summary → 4. Copy or download the result

Example Queries

  • "Summarize this document in 5 bullet points"
  • "What is the total amount on this invoice?"
  • "Extract all line items from this PDF as a table"
  • "What are the key findings in this research paper?"

EchoScribe – Voice Transcription Member

Convert audio recordings to accurate text.

How to Use

1. Click EchoScribe → 2. Upload an audio file or record live → 3. Click Transcribe → 4. Review, copy, or export the text

Tips for Best Accuracy

  • Use clear recordings with minimal background noise
  • Speak at a natural pace — avoid rushing
  • After transcription, paste text into Ask AI for summaries or notes

AI Image Generator Member

Generate images from text descriptions.

How to Use

1. Click Image Generator → 2. Type a detailed description of the image → 3. Click Generate → 4. Download your image

Writing Better Prompts

  • Be specific: "A minimalist logo with a blue robot icon on white background"
  • Include style: "flat design", "realistic photo", "watercolor painting"
  • Specify dimensions or use: "YouTube thumbnail", "LinkedIn banner", "product mockup"

Reply Enchanter – AI Email Writer Member

Paste an email and get a polished AI-drafted reply.

How to Use

1. Click Reply Enchanter → 2. Paste the original email → 3. Select tone (Professional/Friendly/Casual/Urgent) → 4. Click Generate → 5. Copy the reply

Resume Warlock – Interview Prep Member

Upload your resume and receive AI-generated interview questions tailored to your experience.

How to Use

1. Click Resume Warlock → 2. Upload your resume (PDF or Word) → 3. Review the AI-generated questions → 4. Practice answers with Ask AI

Getting the Most Out of Resume Warlock

  • After generating questions, paste them into Ask AI and ask "How should I answer this?"
  • Ask AI to critique your practice answers
  • Request questions for a specific job title: "Generate questions for a Java backend developer role"

REST API Reference

W!ntAi's backend exposes a Spring Boot REST API. The full interactive API documentation is available via Swagger UI.

→ Open Swagger API Docs

Base URL

https://api.wintaibot.com

Authentication

Most endpoints require a Bearer token obtained via login:

POST/api/auth/login

Authorization: Bearer <token>

Key Endpoints

POST/api/ai/ask — AI chatbot
POST/api/ai/reply — Email reply generator
POST/api/ai/analyze — Document analysis
POST/api/ai/transcribe — Voice transcription
POST/api/ai/image — Image generation
POST/api/ai/resume — Interview prep

Built with Spring AI and Java 21. Source available on GitHub.
