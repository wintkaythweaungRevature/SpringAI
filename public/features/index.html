Wintaibot combines PDF analysis, voice transcription, image generation, email writing, interview prep, and AI chat into a single web-based assistant.

📄

DocuWizard – AI PDF & Document Analyzer Member

DocuWizard is Wintaibot's core document AI tool. Upload a PDF, Excel spreadsheet, or Word document and the AI will extract structured data, summarize content, and answer questions about the file — in seconds.

What you can do with DocuWizard:

  • Extract tables from PDF reports and export as Excel
  • Summarize long contracts, research papers, and invoices
  • Ask the AI questions about your document ("What is the total amount due?")
  • Analyze financial statements and highlight key figures
  • Process Word documents (.docx) and Excel files (.xlsx)

Supported formats: PDF · Excel (.xlsx, .xls) · Word (.docx)

🎤

EchoScribe – AI Voice Transcription Member

EchoScribe converts spoken audio into accurate written text using AI speech recognition. Upload a recording or use live audio — EchoScribe transcribes it and optionally summarizes the output.

Best uses for EchoScribe:

  • Transcribe business meetings and client calls
  • Convert university lectures into study notes
  • Create written records of interviews and podcasts
  • Generate subtitles and captions for video content
  • Transcribe voice memos into actionable to-do lists
🖼️

AI Image Generator Member

Turn any text description into a high-quality image using AI. The Wintaibot image generator is powered by a state-of-the-art generative model — describe what you want and get studio-quality visuals in seconds.

Example prompts:

  • "A professional LinkedIn banner for a software developer"
  • "A minimalist logo for a tech startup in blue and white"
  • "An illustration of a robot reading a book, flat design style"
  • "Product mockup of a mobile app on an iPhone 15"
✉️

Reply Enchanter – AI Email Writer Member

Reply Enchanter reads an email you received and drafts a polished, contextually appropriate response. Choose your tone and the AI handles the rest — saving you 5-30 minutes per email.

Tone options:

  • Professional – formal business communication
  • Friendly – warm and approachable
  • Casual – relaxed, informal tone
  • Urgent – direct and action-oriented
📝

Resume Warlock – Interview Preparation AI Member

Upload your resume and Resume Warlock generates AI-tailored interview questions based on your specific experience, skills, and job history. Practice your answers and identify gaps before the real interview.

Resume Warlock helps you:

  • Generate role-specific behavioral and technical questions
  • Identify skills gaps from your resume
  • Practice answering common interview questions with AI feedback
  • Prepare for software engineering, management, and analyst roles
🤖

Ask AI – AI Chatbot Free

Ask AI is Wintaibot's general-purpose AI assistant, available 24/7 for free. Ask any question, get coding help, request writing assistance, or have a conversation — no account required to start.

Common uses:

  • Code debugging and explanation
  • Research and fact-finding
  • Writing and editing assistance
  • Math and logic problem solving
  • Learning new topics with plain-language explanations
🍳

Recipe Generator – AI Meal Planner Free

Enter the ingredients you have and the Recipe Generator will suggest complete, step-by-step recipes using AI. No more wasted food or repetitive meals.

How it works:

  • List your available ingredients
  • Specify dietary preferences (vegetarian, low-carb, etc.)
  • Get multiple recipe suggestions with instructions
  • Discover new cuisines based on what you already own
