Pricing FAQ

Is the free plan really free forever?

Yes. The Ask AI chatbot and Recipe Generator are completely free with no time limit and no credit card required. We will never charge you for the free tier tools.

What payment methods do you accept?

Payments are processed securely by Stripe. We accept all major credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express). We never store your card details.

Can I cancel my subscription?

Yes, cancel anytime from Account Settings inside the app. Your membership stays active until the end of your current billing period. No cancellation fees.

Is there a free trial for the Member plan?

You can use the free tools before subscribing to test the platform. We are working on adding a trial period for the Member plan.

Do you offer team or enterprise plans?

Not yet. Team plans are on our roadmap. Contact us at contact@wintaibot.com if you need bulk access for your organization.
