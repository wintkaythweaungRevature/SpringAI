Pricing FAQ

Is the free plan really free forever?

Yes. The Ask AI chatbot and Recipe Generator are completely free with no time limit and no credit card required. We will never charge you for the free tier tools.

What payment methods do you accept?

Payments are processed securely by Stripe. We accept all major credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express). W!ntAi does not store your bank account information or full card details — only Stripe handles payment and billing data on their PCI-compliant systems.

Can I cancel my subscription?

Yes, cancel anytime from Account Settings inside the app. Your membership stays active until the end of your current billing period. No cancellation fees.

Is there a free trial?

You can use the free tier first. Paid plans bill through Stripe; check the app for any active trial promotions.

Do you offer team plans?

Growth includes 3 team seats (as team features roll out). For larger orgs, email contact@wintaibot.com.
